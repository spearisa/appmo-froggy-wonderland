Why Frogs Are Awesome

Bioindicators

Frogs are sensitive to environmental changes, making them excellent indicators of ecosystem health.

Pest Control

A single frog can eat over 10,000 insects in one season, including mosquitoes and crop pests.

Medical Research

Frog skin contains substances being studied for new antibiotics and painkillers.
